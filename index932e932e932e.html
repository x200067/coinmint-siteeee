About Coinmint

Current position:   Home Page > About Us

Pioneers in Blockchain Infrastructure

Founded in 2015, Coinmint has evolved from a private Bitcoin mining operation for high-net-worth investors to a global leader in blockchain infrastructure, powering the digital economy with sustainable computing power solutions.

Our Story

What began as a private mining venture for institutional investors in 2015 quickly grew into a vision to democratize access to blockchain infrastructure. Responding to demand from our initial investor base, we expanded our platform in 2017 to offer industrial-scale mining solutions to retail investors, leveraging our expertise in energy efficiency and operational excellence.

Today, Coinmint operates state-of-the-art mining facilities in the United States, powered by renewable energy sources, and serves a global community of investors seeking reliable digital asset production.

Our Mission

To build the infrastructure that powers the blockchain economy, connecting energy efficiency with digital asset production to create sustainable value for investors worldwide.

  • Bridge traditional energy resources with cutting-edge blockchain technology
  • Democratize access to digital asset production through scalable infrastructure
  • Advance the adoption of sustainable energy in blockchain operations
  • Provide transparent, reliable, and secure mining solutions for all investor types

Our Vision

To be the preferred global partner for blockchain infrastructure, setting new standards in energy efficiency, operational reliability, and investor accessibility.

Core Values:

  • Integrity: Transparent operations and ethical business practices
  • Excellence: Commitment to technological and operational leadership
  • Sustainability: Environmental responsibility in all operations
  • Accessibility: Making blockchain infrastructure available to all investors
