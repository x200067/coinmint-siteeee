Pioneers in Blockchain Infrastructure

Founded in 2015, Coinmint is a leading provider of high-density computing infrastructure for blockchain networks, specializing in secure and sustainable Bitcoin mining solutions. What began as a private venture for high-net-worth investors has evolved into one of the world's largest digital asset mining operations, powered by innovation and a commitment to clean energy.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to build mining infrastructure that combines technological excellence with environmental responsibility. Today, we operate state-of-the-art data centers that set new standards for efficiency, reliability, and sustainability in the blockchain industry.

Coinmint's unique approach has attracted institutional investors, strategic partners, and top talent from across technology, infrastructure, and finance, enabling us to deliver best-in-class solutions for the digital economy.

Industry-Leading Facilities

Coinmint operates one of the world's largest digital currency data centers in a former Alcoa aluminum smelter in Massena, New York. Our facilities stand out for their scale, efficiency, and commitment to clean energy:

435MW Transformer Capacity

Three times the capacity of any other known digital currency data center, enabling massive scale and operational flexibility.

100% Clean Energy

Powered by hydroelectric energy from the St. Lawrence River, ensuring minimal environmental impact.

Enterprise-Grade Security

24/7 security monitoring, biometric access control, and redundant systems for maximum uptime.

Innovative Cooling Systems

Advanced liquid cooling technology that reduces energy waste by 40% compared to traditional air cooling.

Company Timeline

2015

Coinmint is founded as a private mining venture for high-net-worth investors in New York.

2018

Launch of Massena data center with initial 150MW capacity, powered by hydroelectric energy.

2020

Expansion to 300MW, becoming one of the largest mining facilities in North America.

2023

NYDIG strategic investment, accelerating expansion to 435MW and 100+ PH/s hashrate.

2024

Launch of cloud mining services, making institutional-grade infrastructure accessible to all investors.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To build the infrastructure that powers the future of the digital economy, combining technological innovation with environmental stewardship. We aim to provide secure, efficient, and sustainable blockchain computing solutions that enable widespread adoption of digital assets.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in blockchain infrastructure, setting new standards for scale, efficiency, and sustainability. We envision a world where digital assets are integrated into the global financial system, supported by robust, clean, and accessible computing infrastructure.
