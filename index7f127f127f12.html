How Our Mining Solutions Work

Our mining operations are designed for efficiency, sustainability, and transparency, from infrastructure to income distribution.

1. Infrastructure Setup

We deploy state-of-the-art ASIC miners in our 100% hydroelectric-powered data centers with advanced cooling systems.

2. Hashrate Allocation

Clients purchase or lease hashrate, with their share recorded in our secure management system.

3. Mining Operations

Mining equipment runs 24/7, connecting to top mining pools to validate transactions and generate block rewards.

4. Daily Payouts

Earnings are calculated based on hashrate contribution and distributed daily to client accounts.
