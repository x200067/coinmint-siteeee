CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER & FOUNDER

Ashton Soniat

Energy Trading Power Markets Cryptocurrency Mining Business Development

Ashton Soniat is the visionary founder and CEO of Coinmint, bringing extensive expertise in energy markets and power trading to the cryptocurrency mining industry. With a strong background in electricity trading and business development, he has positioned Coinmint as a leader in sustainable and efficient crypto mining operations.

Professional Background

  • Founded Dufossat Capital, a leading electricity trading firm specializing in the Northeast and Midwest electricity markets, demonstrating deep expertise in power market dynamics and energy infrastructure.
  • Established Coinmint as a premier cryptocurrency mining operation, leveraging strategic partnerships with power providers to optimize mining efficiency and sustainability.
  • Developed innovative approaches to industrial-scale cryptocurrency mining, focusing on renewable energy integration and cost-effective power procurement strategies.
  • Currently serves as a nonresident senior fellow at the Atlantic Council’s Scowcroft Center for Strategy and Security, focusing on emerging technology and global security.
  • Led the transformation of traditional industrial facilities into state-of-the-art cryptocurrency mining operations, maximizing operational efficiency and environmental responsibility.

Education

  • Bachelor of Science from Louisiana State University’s majoring in Political Science.
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Houston, specializing in finance and strategic management with focus on energy markets.

Leadership at Coinmint

As CEO of NCCS, an operating subsidiary of Coinmint, LLC, Ashton leads the company’s strategic direction in transforming industrial infrastructure into cutting-edge cryptocurrency mining facilities. His vision emphasizes sustainable operations, regulatory compliance, and technological innovation, positioning Coinmint as a leader in environmentally responsible crypto mining.

Ashton's expertise blend of private sector entrepreneurship and public sector experience has been instrumental in navigating Coinmint through the complex landscape of blockchain regulation and infrastructure development, ensuring the company remains at the forefront of the global crypto mining industry.
