Hosted Mining

Current position: Home Page > Mining Guide > Hosted Mining

What is Hosted Mining?

Hosted Mining (Hosted Mining Ownership) allows users to purchase Bitcoin or other cryptocurrency mining equipment (such as ASIC miners) and host them in our professional mining facilities. We provide 24/7 power, cooling, network, and maintenance support, while users retain full ownership of the equipment and enjoy all cryptocurrency earnings from its operation.

Unlike cloud mining or contract-based services, hosted mining emphasizes physical asset control and verifiable production, making it an ideal solution for investors seeking tangible ownership and transparent operations.

How Hosted Mining Works

1. Equipment Procurement

Purchase ASIC mining machines (e.g., Bitmain Antminer series) through our platform or certified channels. All equipment is registered under your name for clear ownership.

2. Hosted Deployment

Ship the equipment to our mining facility where we handle power connection, mining pool configuration, cooling setup, and security deployment in our industrial-grade environment.

3. Continuous Operation

Miner operates 24/7 in our controlled environment, automatically participating in Bitcoin network hashing to solve cryptographic puzzles and validate transactions.

4. Output Settlement

We calculate daily earnings (in BTC or USDT) based on the miner's hash power and mining pool performance, depositing directly into your account.

5. Remote Management

Monitor your miner's performance, power consumption, earnings, and efficiency in real-time through our online dashboard with complete data visualization.

Why Choose Hosted Mining?

Physical Asset Ownership

You own the mining equipment, with unique serial numbers and transferable rights, unlike virtual contracts or platform promises.

Transparent Earnings

Earnings come from real mining operations, verifiable on the blockchain, not platform-set returns or simulations.

Professional Operation

We handle all aspects of operation: maintenance, cooling, power, and security, requiring no technical expertise from you.

Cost Efficiency

Benefit from our bulk electricity rates and economies of scale, reducing operational costs compared to individual mining.

Key Benefits of Hosted Mining

No On-Site Infrastructure

Avoid noise, heat, and power load issues by hosting miners in our facilities, keeping your home or office unaffected.

Stable Digital Income

Generate daily BTC earnings, creating a systematic inflow of digital assets suitable for long-term savings or corporate reserve funds.

Fully Managed O&M

Our team handles all maintenance, security, and operational issues, allowing you to participate without technical knowledge.

Complete Transparency

Track every aspect of your miner's performance, from hash rate to electricity usage, with real-time data and detailed reports.

Value Proposition of Hosted Mining

Infrastructure Ownership

Own physical mining infrastructure with clear residual value and transferability, providing tangible asset exposure to cryptocurrency markets.

Real-World Production

Earnings derive from actual blockchain network participation, not financial engineering, providing verifiable and auditable income streams.

Transparent Cost Structure

Known power consumption, electricity rates, and maintenance costs allow precise ROI calculations and financial planning.

Inflation Hedge

Bitcoin's deflationary nature makes hosted mining an effective tool for building inflation-resistant digital asset holdings.

Who Should Consider Hosted Mining?

Individual Investors

Investors with medium-to-long-term asset allocation goals seeking tangible exposure to cryptocurrency through physical infrastructure.

Financial Planners

Professionals looking to include stable BTC production in client portfolios as part of diversified digital asset strategies.

Corporate Investors

Businesses and institutions aiming to build deflationary digital reserves or diversify balance sheets with tangible crypto infrastructure.

Ecosystem Participants

Blockchain enthusiasts transitioning from speculative trading to infrastructure-level participation in the Bitcoin network.
