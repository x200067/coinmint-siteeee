Our Journey from Inception to Leadership

Coinmint's history is marked by strategic vision, technological innovation, and a commitment to sustainable blockchain infrastructure. From our early days as a private mining venture to becoming a leader in institutional-grade mining solutions, here's a timeline of our key milestones:

2015: Founding of Coinmint

Coinmint is established as a private Bitcoin mining venture in New York, focused on providing high-net-worth investors with access to professional mining infrastructure.

2018: Massena Data Center Launch

Opening of our flagship data center in Massena, New York, with initial 150MW capacity, powered by 100% hydroelectric energy from the St. Lawrence River.

2020: Capacity Expansion to 300MW

Significant expansion of our Massena facility to 300MW, establishing Coinmint as one of the largest mining operations in North America.

2023: NYDIG Strategic Investment

NYDIG (New York Digital Investment Group) invests $60 million in Coinmint, accelerating our growth and expansion plans.

2024: Reaching 435MW Capacity

Completion of our Massena facility expansion to 435MW, solidifying our position as a leader in sustainable mining infrastructure.

2025: Launch of Cloud Mining Services

Introduction of our cloud mining platform, making institutional-grade hashrate accessible to retail and institutional investors worldwide.

The Founding of Coinmint

In 2015, a group of technology and infrastructure experts recognized the potential of blockchain technology and the need for professional, sustainable mining solutions. They founded Coinmint with a vision to build mining infrastructure that combines technological excellence with environmental responsibility.

Initially serving high-net-worth private investors, Coinmint quickly established itself as a trusted partner for those seeking secure and efficient access to Bitcoin mining. Our founders understood that long-term success in the industry required a focus on three key pillars:

  • Utilizing clean, renewable energy sources to minimize environmental impact
  • Building scalable infrastructure to meet growing demand
  • Ensuring full regulatory compliance and operational transparency

NYDIG Investment & Growth

2023: NYDIG's Strategic Investment

In 2023, Coinmint secured a $60 million strategic investment from NYDIG (New York Digital Investment Group), a leading institutional digital asset firm. This investment marked a pivotal moment in our history, validating our approach and accelerating our growth trajectory.

Key Impact of the Investment:
  • Accelerated expansion of our Massena facility to 435MW
  • Enabled deployment of 50,000+ next-generation mining units
  • Strengthened our position as a leader in sustainable mining
  • Facilitated the development of our cloud mining platform
NYDIG's Perspective:

"Coinmint stands out for its commitment to clean energy, operational excellence, and regulatory compliance. Their infrastructure represents the future of sustainable blockchain operations, and we're excited to support their growth."

- Robert Gutmann, NYDIG Managing Director
