Coinmint Corporate Strategy

Building the infrastructure for the digital economy through sustainable energy, global expansion, and integrated financial solutions.

Executive Summary

Coinmint is not merely a cryptocurrency brand—we are a global infrastructure company redefining digital asset production and financial services. Headquartered in the United States with operations powered by 95% hydropower, our strategy focuses on three core axes:

Sustainable Infrastructure

Developing industrial-scale mining facilities with clean energy to reduce environmental impact.

Global Compliance

Expanding operations in regulated markets to provide secure, compliant digital asset solutions.

Ecosystem Integration

Creating integrated platforms that connect mining, trading, and financial services seamlessly.

Strategic Pillars

Clean Energy Mining Infrastructure

Our flagship 435 MW hydropower facility in Massena, New York, serves as the foundation of our energy strategy:

  • 130,000+ sq. ft. of industrial mining space
  • 95% hydropower-sourced energy for sustainable operations
  • Long-term power purchase agreement with NYPA
  • Scalable infrastructure to support 100,000+ ASIC miners

This facility sets the standard for environmentally responsible mining, combining high efficiency with low carbon footprint.

Global Cloud Computing Services

Our cloud mining solutions democratize access to blockchain infrastructure:

  • USDT-denominated cloud mining plans for easy entry
  • Automated mining contract management
  • Daily mining revenue distribution
  • Portfolio tracking with real-time hashing power metrics

Designed for novice investors and passive income seekers, our cloud services bridge traditional finance with blockchain technology.

Integrated Trading Ecosystem

Coinmint Exchange combines trading with mining for synergistic value creation:

  • Spot trading for 20+ major cryptocurrencies
  • "Turbo Contracts" with 60-300 second expiration
  • Trade-to-Mine Bridge for automatic profit reinvestment
  • Institutional-grade API for algorithmic trading

Our trading platform is not just a marketplace—it's a gateway to integrate trading profits with long-term mining income.

Digital Financial Infrastructure

Expanding beyond mining to build comprehensive digital asset services:

  • Crypto lending against mining assets
  • Portfolio management tools for diversified strategies
  • Integration with Web3 applications and NFT ecosystems
  • Institutional custody and asset management solutions

We aim to become a one-stop shop for digital asset investors, from retail users to large institutions.

Global Expansion Roadmap

Completed Milestones

2015-2017: Founded as private mining operation, launched first cloud mining services.

2019: Reached 100 MW capacity, established hydropower partnership with NYPA.

2021: Secured $50M funding from NYDIG, expanded to 300 MW.

2023: Launched integrated trading platform, achieved 435 MW sustainable capacity.

Current Initiatives (2025)

Q2: Launch crypto lending services with mining assets as collateral.

Q4: Introduce NFT-based mining ownership certificates.

Ongoing: Expand institutional custody services and API solutions.

Future Strategy (2026+)

2026: Establish operations in Asia and Europe with localized compliance.

2026+: Scale institutional services: custody, API, quantitative tools.

Long-Term: Integrate with Web3 ecosystems: gaming, DeFi, green nodes.

Strategic Differentiators

Sustainable Energy

95% hydropower utilization, far exceeding industry average

Regulatory Compliance

US-based operations with FinCEN registration and KYC/AML protocols

Ecosystem Integration

Seamless connection between trading, mining, and financial services

Operational Excellence

Industrial-scale facilities with 99.9% uptime and advanced cooling systems

Strategic Vision

Coinmint is not chasing short-term trends—we are building the infrastructure for the future of digital finance. Our strategy is guided by three core beliefs:

  1. Sustainability is non-negotiable: The future of blockchain depends on environmentally responsible infrastructure.
  2. Compliance drives adoption: Regulated markets will be the primary growth engine for digital assets.
  3. Integration creates value: Seamless connections between mining, trading, and financial services will define the next generation of digital platforms.

By adhering to these principles, Coinmint aims to be the trusted infrastructure partner for the decentralized economy, providing a robust and sustainable foundation for digital asset innovation.
