The Origin of Coinmint

Current position: Home Page > Our History > The Origin of Coinmint

From Abandoned Factory to Global Mining Hub

Trace the journey of how Coinmint transformed an abandoned industrial site in upstate New York into a pioneering blockchain infrastructure hub powered by clean energy.

2015

The Birth of a Vision

Despite Bitcoin price fluctuations in 2015, Coinmint committed $700 million to the project, believing in the long-term value of blockchain technology.

2016

Infrastructure Development

The transformation began, facing the challenge of converting old industrial facilities into a high-tech computing center:

  • Upgraded power systems to support 435 MW of computing capacity
  • Designed efficient cooling systems leveraging the region's cold climate
  • Installed infrastructure for the first 100,000 ASIC miners

The engineering team innovatively utilized the factory's structure to maximize miner deployment while maintaining energy efficiency.

2017

Clean Energy Strategy

Coinmint signed a long-term agreement with NYPA, securing 95% hydropower for its operations, establishing a clean energy mining strategy:

  • 20-year hydropower purchase agreement for sustainable energy supply
  • Intelligent energy management system to optimize hash rate-to-energy ratio
  • One of the first large-scale mines to adopt 100% renewable energy

This strategic choice established Coinmint as an industry leader in environmentally responsible mining.

2018

Launch of a Global Mining Hub

Coinmint announced the completion of its 435 MW mining center, becoming the world's largest Bitcoin mining facility at the time:

  • First 50,000 miners deployed, achieving 1.2 EH/s hashing power
  • Created 150 local jobs, stimulating regional economic growth
  • Established industry-standard processes for miner operation and management

The Massena facility marked Coinmint's transformation from startup to a key player in blockchain infrastructure.

2019-Present

From Mining to Ecosystem

Building on the success of the Massena facility, Coinmint expanded its ecosystem:

  • 2019: Launched cloud mining services to democratize access
  • 2021: Completed $50M funding round to scale operations
  • 2023: Launched trading platform, creating a "mining+trading" ecosystem

From an abandoned factory to a comprehensive ecosystem, Coinmint's journey reflects the evolution of blockchain infrastructure from niche to mainstream.
