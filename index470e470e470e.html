Coinmint Intellectual Property Statement

At Coinmint, intellectual property is more than legal compliance—it's the cornerstone of our innovation strategy and global trust. This statement outlines our IP framework, protection mechanisms, and user guidelines.

Important Notice

Coinmint is not affiliated with any entity named "Coinmint" in India. All transactions or contractual arrangements with coinmint.in should be directed to that entity. Coinmint's intellectual property is independently owned and protected under US and international law.

IP Strategy Overview

Coinmint's intellectual property strategy is built on three pillars:

  • Legal protection of proprietary technologies and brand identity
  • Technical safeguards to prevent unauthorized use
  • Strategic IP management to drive innovation

Our IP portfolio supports our position as a leading blockchain infrastructure provider, covering everything from source code to brand assets.

Scope of Intellectual Property

The following assets are protected under our IP framework:

  • Software Assets: Platform source code, smart contracts, algorithm engines
  • Brand Assets: "Coinmint" trademark, logo, visual identity system
  • Content Assets: White papers, technical documentation, marketing materials
  • Design Assets: Website interfaces, user experience designs, data visualizations
  • Business Assets: Proprietary business models, financial analytics frameworks

Ownership & Usage Terms

All intellectual property on the Coinmint platform is owned by Energy & Compute LLC, dba Coinmint, including works created by employees and partners within the scope of their engagement.

User Usage Restrictions:

  • Limited permission for personal use of platform services
  • Prohibition on re-distribution, modification, or commercial exploitation
  • No right to sublicense, sell, or transfer Coinmint IP assets
  • Requirement to maintain IP attribution for all authorized uses

IP Protection Mechanisms

Coinmint employs a multi-layered protection strategy:

  • Legal Protection: Trademark registration with USPTO (Serial No. 97854321), copyright filings
  • Technical Protection: Code obfuscation, access controls, digital rights management
  • Contractual Protection: IP assignment agreements with employees and partners
  • Monitoring Systems: Automated DMCA takedown processes, brand abuse detection
  • International Protection: IP rights registered in key markets (US, EU, Singapore)

Third-Party IP Compliance

Coinmint maintains strict compliance with third-party intellectual property rights, operating under the following principles:

Compliance Area Policy Details Legal Framework
Open Source Usage All open source components (MIT, GPL, Apache) are used in compliance with license terms, with proper attribution. Open Source Initiative (OSI) standards
Third-Party Resources External APIs, libraries, and media assets are used within authorized commercial terms. Terms of Service (ToS) of respective providers
Copyright Infringement Adheres to the US Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) for takedown requests. 17 U.S.C. § 512 (DMCA)
Trademark Usage Avoids infringing on registered trademarks of third parties in all operations. Lanham Act (15 U.S.C. § 1051 et seq.)

User Submission Policy

Coinmint does not accept unsolicited ideas, suggestions, or creative submissions. By voluntarily submitting any content, users agree to the following terms:

  • Waiver of all intellectual property rights to the submitted content
  • Coinmint's right to use, modify, distribute, or commercialize the content
  • No obligation for confidentiality, attribution, or compensation
  • Release of all claims related to the submitted content

IP Enforcement

In the event of suspected intellectual property infringement, Coinmint may take the following actions:

  • Issue cease and desist letters
  • Initiate legal proceedings for infringement
  • Seek injunctive relief and damages
  • Engage in administrative proceedings (e.g., UDRP for domain name disputes)
  • Implement technical measures to prevent further infringement
