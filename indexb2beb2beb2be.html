Strategic Timeline

2015-2017

Founded as private mining operation for high-net-worth investors. Launched first cloud mining services in response to demand.

2019

Reached 100 MW capacity, established long-term hydropower partnership with NYPA, expanded facility to 50,000 sq. ft.

2021

Secured $50M funding from NYDIG, expanded to 300 MW, introduced institutional-grade hosting solutions.

2023

Launched integrated trading platform with Trade-to-Mine Bridge, achieved 435 MW sustainable capacity, served 25,000+ investors.

2025

Launch crypto lending against mining assets, introduce NFT-based mining certificates, expand API services for institutions.

2026

Establish operations in Asia and Europe with localized compliance, scale custody solutions for institutional clients.

2027+

Integrate with Web3 ecosystems (gaming, DeFi, green nodes), become leading global blockchain infrastructure provider.

Strategic Differentiation

Sustainability Leadership

95% hydropower utilization far exceeds industry average, with plans to achieve 100% renewable energy by 2026.

  • Long-term PPA with NYPA for clean energy
  • Carbon footprint 80% lower than industry standard
  • Recipient of NY State Clean Energy Award

Regulatory Compliance

US-based operations with FinCEN registration, full KYC/AML compliance, and ongoing regulatory consultation.

  • Registered Money Services Business (MSB)
  • Annual independent compliance audits
  • GDPR and FATCA compliant operations

Ecosystem Integration

Unique integration of mining, trading, and financial services creating synergistic value for investors.

  • Trade-to-Mine Bridge for automatic profit reinvestment
  • Unified portfolio management across services
  • Seamless fund flow between mining and trading accounts

Strategic Vision

Coinmint is building the infrastructure for the next generation of the digital economy. Our vision is guided by three core principles:

Sustainable Blockchain

We believe blockchain technology should drive innovation without environmental compromise, leading with clean energy solutions.

Global Accessibility

Democratizing access to blockchain infrastructure, making digital asset production available to investors worldwide.

Integrated Ecosystem

Creating a seamless ecosystem where mining, trading, and financial services work together to maximize investor value.
