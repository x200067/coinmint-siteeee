Coinmint Cloud Mining Lease

Invest in real hashing power, receive daily payouts, and build a structured digital income model with Coinmint's industrial-grade mining infrastructure.

Project Overview

Cloud mining lease allows users to participate in Bitcoin mining by renting hashing power resources (e.g., 10 TH/s) in Coinmint's industrial mines, without purchasing physical miners. Earn daily BTC or USDT based on actual network output.

Our solution is built on Coinmint's self-owned physical mine in Massena, New York, leveraging hydropower, industrial-grade mining equipment, and advanced engineering systems to provide a transparent, hassle-free digital asset investment channel.

What You're Investing In

  • US-based industrial mining facilities with long-term power contracts
  • Operational mining equipment connected to global mining pools
  • Transparent on-chain output model with daily income settlement
  • A "digital value factory" driven by hardware, energy, and system efficiency

Investment Advantages

  1. Physical Asset Backed – Your hashing power corresponds to real mining hardware, not simulated contracts
  2. Daily Payouts – Earn BTC or USDT daily based on actual hashing performance
  3. Volatility Resistant – Income derived from network blocks, not short-term price fluctuations
  4. No Technical Expertise Needed – We handle mining pool management, equipment maintenance, and energy optimization
  5. Flexible Investment – Suitable for both stable income seekers and long-term BTC accumulation strategies

How It Works

Rent hashing power → Coinmint operates mining infrastructure → System calculates daily earnings → Payouts in BTC or USDT → Withdraw or reinvest freely

Income Structure

Coinmint uses a transparent real-output model for earnings calculation:

Daily Net Income = Your Hashing Power Share × Network Block Rewards × BTC Price - Operational Costs

  • All calculations based on real-time on-chain data
  • Performance metrics viewable in user dashboard
  • No guaranteed returns – earnings based on actual system output
  • 2024 historical annualized return range: 12-20% (market-dependent)
