Our Clean Energy Commitment

Coinmint is dedicated to building a sustainable digital infrastructure ecosystem. We understand that future financial returns must be rooted in environmental responsibility, which is why all our mining facilities are powered by clean energy and operated in strict accordance with ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) principles.

Energy Source Breakdown

100% Hydroelectric-Powered Mining

Our flagship mining facility in Massena, New York, is powered entirely by hydroelectric energy from the St. Lawrence River, setting new standards for sustainable blockchain infrastructure.

Energy Source & Efficiency

  • Long-term power purchase agreement with the New York Power Authority (NYPA)
  • Stable energy cost of $0.03/kWh—30% lower than the global mining average
  • 435MW transformer capacity with room for expansion
  • Advanced liquid cooling systems reducing energy waste by 40%

Environmental Impact

  • Zero carbon emissions—no coal, natural gas, or fuel oil used
  • Carbon footprint 95% lower than traditional mining operations
  • No contribution to resource competition or power shortages
  • Third-party audited energy consumption reports available

Coinmint's Massena facility harnessing hydroelectric power from the St. Lawrence River

ESG Framework: Environment, Social, Governance

Coinmint's operations adhere to the three pillars of ESG, ensuring responsible business practices and long-term sustainability.

Environment (E)

No harmful emissions or heavy metal residues. Intelligent cooling systems minimize energy waste, with plans to integrate solar/wind hybrid modules.

Social (S)

Created 300+ local jobs in upstate New York. Collaborates with local governments on infrastructure development and provides skills training programs.

Governance (G)

Fully compliant with U.S. regulatory frameworks. Auditable financial and energy data. No use of platform coins or non-compliant asset models.
